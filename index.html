IDReady Checker

Check the status of your National ID


How to Use This Website

Welcome to IDReady Checker — a simple tool that helps you know whether your National ID is ready without visiting the chief’s office.

  • Step 1: Enter your serial number in the input box above.
  • Step 2: Click Check Status.
  • Step 3: Wait for the result to appear.

Ready: Your ID is available for collection.

Waiting: Your ID is still being processed.

Error: ID not found or wrong serial number.
